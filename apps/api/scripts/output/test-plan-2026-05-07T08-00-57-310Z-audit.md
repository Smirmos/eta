## Significant issues

1. **D/ME4 prerequisite not met (Week 9).** Plan introduces D/ME4 (threshold run) in week 9 with rationale "consolidates ME after multiple cruise interval sessions (prerequisite met)." But D/ME4 explicitly requires "at least four of the other ME interval workouts" before attempting (KB `03-workouts.md` p. 472, line 711). Tracing back through `keySessions`, the only prior run-ME is D/ME1 in week 11 — that is one workout, not four. Either schedule additional D/ME interval workouts in weeks 13–10 (the Pass-2 detail might add them, but it cannot be assumed) or replace D/ME4 in week 9 with another D/ME1 session. The "(prerequisite met)" parenthetical is unsupported by the JSON itself.

2. **Two zero-swim weeks (7 and 5) in Build 2 for a swim-weak athlete.** Verified: `keySessions` for weeks 7 and 5 contain 0 swim entries and 3 bike entries each (week 7: E/ME1, C/ME3, C/AE2 + 1 run D/ME1; week 5: E/ME1, C/ME1, C/AE2 + 1 run D/ME1). Athlete's swim is 15 % of historical volume and the longest recent swim (3 000 m) is below IM distance (3 800 m). The plan notes do **not** justify dropping the swim in these weeks. KB `04-weekly-templates.md` rule 22 (p. 214) recommends "at least three workouts in each sport weekly" for highly experienced triathletes; a Build-2 week with a single brick + no isolated swim leaves no room for swim sharpening at the moment IM-specific work peaks. Caveat: `keySessions` are 1–2 breakthrough workouts/day, not the full weekly schedule, and Pass-2 detail could add easy swim sessions — but a *breakthrough*-grade swim is missing in the very weeks the athlete most needs it. Recommend adding B/ME1 or B/ME2 to weeks 7 and 5 keySessions.

3. **Week-15 long-ride / long-run placement violates athlete profile and Friel rule 25 / 28.** Week 15 notes: "Saturday long ride, Sunday medium long run." Profile says `longSessionDays = ['fri','sun']` — Saturday is *not* a long-session day. Beyond the profile mismatch, the Sat-ride / Sun-run pattern is the **wrong-headed pattern Friel explicitly criticizes** ("a long run on Sunday following a Saturday long ride increases your risk for an injury while lowering the quality of your run", KB `04-weekly-templates.md` rule 26 / p. 232). Rule 28 says if both must share the weekend, do long run Sat / long ride Sun. Week 14 onward correctly uses Fri (long run) + Sun (long ride), so the breach is just week 15. Recommend moving week-15 long ride to Sunday and long run to Friday (matching the rest of the plan).
